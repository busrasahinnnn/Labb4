It's Time to Know Me

Posted on May 25, 2024 by Busra

Hey all my name is Busra, I was a elementary school teacher in Turkey. It was such a fun job for me. I did it for 5 years after my graduation from university. I enjoyed my job a lot but at one day I wanted to change everything in my life. I quit from to job and started looking for another county to go. Guess what I chose Canada. I came to Canada at 2019, my whole life was changed. I worked as a babysitter at the beginning to start my journey in Canada. I worked with 3 different families, then I did Uber and some delivery jobs for a while. After all I started working as a bartender at Portuguese pub. It was a huge change in my life being a bartender from elementary school, I still prefer children to drunk old guys.
